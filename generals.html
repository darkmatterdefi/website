Generals

Meet Them

BIEL

Cold Army

Biel The Metallurgist is Super Strong and was born on the metal rich planet of Phólos located in the outer limits of an unknown Galaxy. Phóleons specialize in metallurgy and provide the Cold Army with military grade weapons and resources for building materials. Biel is known to wear an indesctructable plate of armor made up of material only he can forge.

Jobless

Cold Army

Jobless The Sniper is renown for his work on the battlefield, while he lacks some brains and sense, he makes up for it with high agility and accuracy with a rifle. Probably more concerned about what's for lunch than who he has to shoot. Mmmm maybe pizza.

Doctor 'Doc" Qi

Hot Army

Doc Qi was the first healer on the battlefield, dedicated to treating those hurt after discovering Dark Energy could be used for amazing healing powers. Doc founded a school for medicine and began his teachings to his students who regularly called him The Healer. As much as he likes to heal people, he knows when it's important to make them hurt.

Hakuna

Hot Army

Hakuna was the first to master the art of ranged combat, creating his first Bow and Arrows from the wood of the Singing Trees of Planet Ayulezi. After The Great Perish of Sector Seven, The Archer upgraded his tools using a varous range of powerful metals. With the help of Hakuna, the Hot Army is able to thin out waves of attackers before they can even get close.

Inyur

Hot Army

Inyur, The Mage for the Hot Army, has been studying and conjuring many spells and sorceries over the ages. During the Early Era he was crowned for his honorable stand against Peruthian, at the cost of losing his men and being forever energy scarred acoss his body. He continues to serve and protect his fellow brothers, now studying protective magic extensively.

Hazey

Hot Army

Hazey figured out he the blood of the Founders on accident, in a moment of fear learned the Dark Energy flowed through his spray cans as he accidentally sprayed a constable across the wall like paint. Shortly after being arrested, Stark heard of the incident and sent for him, and after ages of being on his own, he was welcomed to the Founders Table as The Painter.

Mxri

Cold Army

Mxri never understood the early way of battle and wanted something more efficient for the wars to come. As The Enforcer for the Cold Crew, Mxri pushed to create the perfect weapon for blasting away those Hot Scum. Harnessing the power of Dark Energy, he created the Dark Shot, scattering shards and balls of Energy to cut down his enemies with ease.

X

Hot Army

X was a prisoner of the Cold Army, working as mechanic on a battleship during the Quiet Era. The early containment of Dark Energy was sometimes unpredicatble, leading to an explosion that sent X out into the void of space. Luckily, he was found and Doc Qi was able to heal him, though forever imbedded in his neck would be the The Eye that somehow came from that incident.

Cyglaxion

Cold Army

Cyglaxion is the most trusted transporter for the Cold Army, though most know him as simply The Pilot. Whether carrying precious cargo or gunning down enemy aircraft, he's the one for the job with above stellar flight ratings. The few that were lucky to shoot down his aircraft were disappointed to learn he made it out unscathed with the help of his quantum glove.

Stark

Hot Army

Stark got his start in the Early Era on the battlefield commanding his own troops to victory in numorous battles. It wasn't until the Discovery Era that he settled down to became The Scientist, heading up multiple research projects to push the advancements of the Hot Army. His life was forever changed when he lost the ones closest to him.

Masnaka

Cold Army

Masnaka has been the head engineer for the Cold Army since the Creation Era, building a foundation and leading the Builders to success in developing early infastructure and Dark Energy storage. The Captain of the Builders, Masnaka weilds his trusty Zeraluct Shield and Dark Wrench to defend his men and conquer on the field of battle.

Rawana

Cold Army

The Samurai has lived an honorable and quiet life, reflecting on his past and times warring with the Cold Army. He was spending his days living alone on a micro planet lacking energy, studying philosophy and living in peace in simplicity. The quiet was ended as a ship touched down and a messenger came forth, presenting his Katana and calling upon him once more.

Orclad

Cold Army

Orclad has always been an outcast of the Cold Army, as a loner that has always made bad decisions, he became The Degen and turned to selling stardust on the black market. He was recruited by the Hot Army during the Truce Era to spy against the Cold, but even as the outsider he was always loyal to his brothers and sisters and turned double agent.

Mety

Cold Army

Mety, or as his enemies call him, The Cyborg, was gravely wounded in a small conflict during the Truce Era. While Cold scientists were able to bring him back from near death, he forever lost a piece of himself at the Battle of Vunmablar. With the help of his new robotic equipment, he vows to let nothing stand between him and stopping the Hot Army.

Gwei-Fi

Hot Army

Gwei retired from battle and spent his time during the Truce Era travelling beyond the edges of the plotted Sectors to continue the Map of the Universe. Along the way he would stop at empty planets along the rim to scout viablity for greater infastructure. Preferring the life of The Nomad, Gwei wasn't excited to learn war had broke out again, ruining his plans.

Artadokht

Hot Army

Agile and fierce, The Onna-Musha is the last of the female Founders remaining in battle, gliding and dancing across the field, cutting her opponents down with her trusty, energy-infused naginata. Atradokht studied under Kilesha in the Early Era and follows her code of living, spending her time outside of battlefield helping shape and govern galactic law.

Burner

Hot Army

He was the only unnamed Founder during the Creation Era, though he was more notorious for his fascination with fire and setting things into a purple inferno; usually at the demise of those around him. So they gave him the name Burner, and he continued to set ablaze anything that pleased him, though now he is commonly known as The Pyro.
